Roadmap

Phase 1

PREPARATION

(3 months)

Phase 1 focuses on building technical and social infrastructure. The goal is to drive hype for the project develop our community, and prepare for the Initial minting

  • Launch website
  • Launch Discord and startcommunity building
  • Marketing and drive hype
  • Whitelisting
  • Minting

Phase 2

SAFETY

(3 months)

Phase 2 focuses on protecting those who took the leap of faith in our project. we are so conﬁdent in our ability to succeed that we will offer 100% refund to those of you who changed your minds before the reveal. we also have revolutionary feature “inheritance”. which will give even more protection in case the NFT market goes catastrophic.

  • 100% refund period
  • Reveal
  • inheritance feature
  • Whitelisting
  • Retirement feature

Phase 3

GROWTH

(3 months)

Phase 3 is when we go hard. with a strong foundation built and a thriving community, we will heavily invest proﬁts from the initial miniting on marketing and partnerships with other projects in preparation for our second collection (which will be free to mint for gensis holders).

  • Marketing
  • Secret feature #1
  • Form alliances with other NFT projects
  • Secret feature #2 (HINT :/ﬂex)
  • Preparation for 2nd NFT collection
  • Secret feature #3 (requires genesis NFT)
  • Secret feature #4 (with partnership)
  • Mint ends for 2nd collection
  • 3D avatars
  • Reveal

Phase 4

COMMUNITY & ART NFT COLLECTION

(6 months)

Phase 4 we focus on two primary value drivers. One is continious re-invesestment into the community this means giveaways, airdrops, partner perks and improving the value of our ecosystem. Two is to expand into non utility driven NFTs that maintains value in the long term.

  • Marketing
  • Partnership with talented artist(s)
  • Release art NFT collection
  • Community Treasury
  • Community voting
  • Weekly giveaways
  • Partnerships networks

Phase 5

METAVERSE

(5 years)

Phase 5 is when we step into our long-term vision to participate in the metaverse evolution . The metaverse is a convergence of the physical world and digital world.
